Research (Cem Bozsahin)

I work at the intersection of computer science, linguistics, philosophy and cognitive science, studying complex organized behavior of the mind arising from simple mechanisms for it. So far, I've been able to look at language and daily planning, studying them with grammars as the mechanism.

Updates and new uploads of research output are in Academia.edu.

Old manuscripts are at my METU research page.
