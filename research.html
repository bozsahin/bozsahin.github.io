Research (Cem Bozsahin)

I work at the intersection of computer science, linguistics, philosophy and cognitive science, studying complex organized behavior of the mind trying to see if it arises from simple mechanisms. So far, I've been able to look at language and daily planning, studying them with grammar as the mechanism.

Updates and new uploads of research output are in Academia.edu.

Old manuscripts are at my METU research page.
